Welcome to Lakeside!

Howard's Lakeside General Store in Springfield, OH is a one-stop shop for all your fishing and hunting needs. Offering a wide range of live bait, tackle, fishing and hunting licenses, camping supplies, firewood, drinks, and snacks, this local store is a go-to destination for outdoor enthusiasts.


With their convenient reel respooling service and a variety of live bait options including redworms, night crawlers, leeches, and live fish, Howard's Lakeside General Store ensures that anglers have everything they need for a successful fishing trip. Whether you're a seasoned fisherman or a beginner, this store has you covered with their extensive selection of bait and fishing supplies.
